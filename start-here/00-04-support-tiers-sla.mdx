# Support Tiers & SLA

## AceMQ support tiers

| Tier | Response SLA | Channels | Scope |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Standard** | Next business day | Email, portal | RabbitMQ configuration, best practices |
| **Priority** | 4 hours (business hours) | Email, portal, Slack | All Standard + architecture review, upgrade planning |
| **Embedded** | 1 hour (24/7) | All channels + dedicated Slack | All Priority + embedded SRE support, on-call coverage |

## How to reach support

- **Portal:** Submit a ticket at the AceMQ support portal
- **Slack:** Priority and Embedded customers have a dedicated Slack channel
- **Email:** support@acemq.com

## What AceMQ support covers

- RabbitMQ configuration review and tuning
- Cluster upgrade planning and assistance
- Performance analysis and optimization
- Security configuration review
- Incident response and root cause analysis
- Architecture design review

## Escalation

For Embedded tier customers, AceMQ provides direct escalation to Broadcom/VMware support for bugs in the RabbitMQ core.
